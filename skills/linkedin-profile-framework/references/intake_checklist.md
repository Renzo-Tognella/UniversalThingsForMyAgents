# Intake Checklist

Use this checklist before analysis. If an item is missing, infer only when the risk is low.

## Must Collect

- CV or PDF
- exported LinkedIn profile
- user-supplied sources and notes
- target role or target roles
- current stack and main technologies
- years of experience or career stage
- location / market geography
- industries or domains worked in
- projects with evidence
- links to GitHub, portfolio, blog, or case studies
- constraints: confidentiality, NDA, weak points, job search mode

## High-Value Questions

- What type of recruiter should this profile attract?
- What should be obvious in 10 seconds?
- What should be de-emphasized or removed?
- What proof is available today?
- What proof still needs to be built?

## Domain Intelligence Inputs

Build this map before recommendations:

- main technologies
- adjacent technologies
- role-specific terminology
- recruiter search keywords
- maturity signals for the domain
- portfolio artifacts that matter
- market trends relevant to this career path
- hype signals to ignore

## Output Guards

- Do not generalize across domains without evidence.
- Do not treat a CV and a LinkedIn profile as the same artifact.
- Do not recommend skills without explaining why they matter now.
- Do not rewrite before diagnosis is complete.
